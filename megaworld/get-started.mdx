These tools are added to GameObjects in your scene. They only require the **Working with Data** foundation.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Terrain Spawner" icon="tree" href="/megaworld/getting-started/terrain-spawner">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Texture Stamper" icon="paintbrush" href="/megaworld/getting-started/texture-stamper">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Gravity Spawner" icon="arrow-down" href="/megaworld/getting-started/gravity-spawner">
