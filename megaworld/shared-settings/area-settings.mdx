---

Area Settings define the scene-space bounds used by MegaWorld stamper tools.

This block controls the shape, size, and visualization of the stamper volume.

## Core fields

The current Area Settings code includes:

- **Bounds**
- **Color Cube**
- **Dotted**
- **Draw Handle If Not Selected**
- **Handle Settings Mode**
- **Pixel Width**

## What Area Settings do

- store the current stamper bounds;
- update those bounds when the stamper transform changes;
- provide the box area used by spawning and filtering logic;
- support scene visualization and handle drawing.

## Extra utility actions

The area system also supports fitting the stamper volume to:

- the active terrain size
- the world or scene bounds

## Where Area Settings are used

Area Settings are a core part of stamper tools such as:

- [Terrain Spawner](/megaworld/tools/terrain-spawner)
- [Texture Stamper](/megaworld/tools/texture-stamper)
- [Gravity Spawner](/megaworld/tools/gravity-spawner)

<Tip>
  Before tuning scatter or filters on a stamper tool, confirm the area bounds first. A wrong area volume makes every downstream setting harder to read.
</Tip>
