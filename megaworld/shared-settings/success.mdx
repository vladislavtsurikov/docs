---
title: "Success"
description: "Control how strongly a prototype competes during random prototype selection"
---

`Success` controls how likely a prototype is to win when MegaWorld chooses between multiple active prototypes.

This is the current component behind the older wording sometimes described as additional spawn weighting.

## Setting

- **Success (%)** sets the prototype’s selection strength.

## Behavior

- Higher values make the prototype more competitive.
- Lower values make it appear less often relative to other active prototypes.
- A value of `100` gives it full weight.

## Where it is used

This setting appears in many object-based tools, including:

- [Terrain Spawner](/megaworld/tools/terrain-spawner)
- [Advanced Brush](/megaworld/tools/advanced-brush)
- [Gravity Spawner](/megaworld/tools/gravity-spawner)
- [Precise Place](/megaworld/tools/precise-place)

<Note>
  Use `Success` to control prototype frequency before touching scatter density. It is often the cleanest way to balance variants within one group.
</Note>
