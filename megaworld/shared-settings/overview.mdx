Shared Settings are the reusable building blocks that show up across multiple MegaWorld tools.

Learning these once makes the rest of MegaWorld much easier, because many tools reuse the same concepts with only small differences.

## Included settings

- [Brush Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/brush-settings)
- [Layer Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/layer-settings)
- [Area Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/area-settings)
- [Random Seed Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/random-seed-settings)
- [Success](/megaworld/shared-settings/success)
- [Simple Transform Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/simple-transform-settings)
- [Physics Transform Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/physics-transform-settings)
- [Simple Filter Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/simple-filter-settings)

## What this group covers

- editor interaction, such as brushes and areas
- repeatability, such as random seeds
- acceptance and failure behavior, such as success tuning
- lightweight transform and filter workflows used by several tools

## Why this group matters

If two tools feel similar, the reason is often here. They may use different placement logic but still share the same brush system, seed logic, or transform behavior.

<Note>
  When you learn a new tool, scan this group first. It usually explains the parts that repeat from workflow to workflow.
</Note>
