---
title: "Physics Effects Settings"
description: "Configure force magnitude and direction randomness applied by PhysX Painter tools"
---

Physics Effects Settings define how force is applied to scene objects during physics interactions.

They are shared across PhysX Painter tools that push, attract, or lift existing objects.

## Force

Controls the strength of the effect.

- **Force Range** — when enabled, MegaWorld picks a random force value between **Min Force** and **Max Force** for each affected object. Enabled by default.
- **Min Force** — the lower bound of the force range. When **Force Range** is disabled, this value is used as the fixed force.
- **Max Force** — the upper bound of the force range.

## Direction

- **Random Strength** — controls the direction of the applied force as a lerp between straight down `(0, -1, 0)` and a random horizontal direction. At `0` the force points directly downward. At `100` the force points in a fully random horizontal direction. Values in between blend the two. Default is `50`.
