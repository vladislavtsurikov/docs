---
title: "Physics Effects Settings"
description: "Configure force magnitude and direction randomness applied by PhysX Painter tools"
---

Physics Effects Settings define how force is applied to scene objects during physics interactions.

They are shared across PhysX Painter tools that push, attract, or lift existing objects.

## Force

Controls the strength of the effect.

- **Force Range** — enables a minimum and maximum range. When enabled, MegaWorld picks a random value between **Min Force** and **Max Force** for each affected object.
- **Min Force** — the lower bound of the force range.
- **Max Force** — the upper bound of the force range.

When **Force Range** is disabled, a single fixed force value is used instead of a range.

## Direction

Controls how much randomness is added to the force direction.

- **Random Strength** — adds a random offset to the direction the force is applied. A value of `0` applies force in a fixed direction. Higher values scatter the direction more.
