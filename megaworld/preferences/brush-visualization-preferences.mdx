---
title: "Brush visualization preferences"
description: "Adjust how brush handles look in the MegaWorld scene view"
---

Brush Visualization Preferences control how MegaWorld draws brush handles in the scene view.

<Warning>
  This is an advanced user setting. These settings only affect appearance, not brush behavior.
</Warning>

## Settings

### Circle Color

**Default:** a medium blue — RGB `(0.2, 0.5, 0.7)`

The color of the brush circle drawn in the scene view. Change this if the default color blends into your terrain or skybox.

### Circle Pixel Width

**Default:** `5`  
**Range:** `1–5`

The stroke thickness of the brush circle outline in screen pixels. Use a higher value if the circle is hard to see on high-resolution monitors or against busy terrain.

### Draw Solid Disc

**Default:** `true`

When enabled, MegaWorld fills the brush area with a semi-transparent disc in addition to the circle outline. Disable this if you prefer a clean outline only, or if the filled area makes it harder to see the terrain underneath.

## Effect on Brush Settings

These preferences affect only how the brush looks. The actual brush size, spacing, and mask are controlled in [Brush Settings](/megaworld/shared-settings/brush-settings).
