---
title: "Poisson Disc"
description: "Generate evenly spaced samples by enforcing a minimum distance between them"
---

Poisson Disc creates a more even natural distribution by enforcing a minimum spacing between samples.

This is often a strong choice for vegetation, props, or any placement where you want a natural spread without obvious grids.

## Setting

- **Poisson Disc Size** controls the minimum spacing between samples.

## When to use Poisson Disc

Poisson Disc is best when you want:

- even spacing without visible grid lines;
- natural-looking object spread;
- fewer clumps and fewer accidental near-overlaps at the sample stage.

## Notes

- Poisson Disc is usually more controlled than Random Point.
- If you want hard structure, use [Random Grid](/megaworld/scatter-system/random-grid) instead.
- If you want even spacing but still some variation, Poisson Disc is often the best starting point.
