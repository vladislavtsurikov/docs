---

General Settings are the shared configuration blocks that shape how objects are created after a valid placement point is found.

These settings are not the same as filters, transform stacks, or overlap checks. They sit closer to the actual spawn result.

## Included settings

- [Spawn Detail Settings](/megaworld/general-settings/spawn-detail-settings)
- [Terrain Detail Settings](/megaworld/general-settings/terrain-detail-settings)
- [Additional Spawn Settings](/megaworld/general-settings/additional-spawn-settings)

## How to think about this group

- Use **Spawn Detail Settings** when the workflow needs density-style control over spawned detail.
- Use **Terrain Detail Settings** when the result targets terrain detail resources.
- Use **Additional Spawn Settings** when you need extra behavior around the final spawn result.

## Where these settings appear

You will most often meet this group in tools such as:

- [Terrain Spawner](/megaworld/tools/terrain-spawner)
- [Advanced Brush](/megaworld/tools/advanced-brush)
- [Gravity Spawner](/megaworld/tools/gravity-spawner)

<Note>
  Tune general settings after you are happy with filtering and placement logic. They work best when the candidate positions are already correct.
</Note>
