**Pin** is an Editor Window tool that marks specific placed objects so they are protected from being modified or erased by other MegaWorld tools. Use Pin to lock hero objects, manually placed focal props, or any object you don't want accidentally overwritten during a broad brush or spawn pass.

---

## Step 1 — Open the MegaWorld Window and select Pin

Press **Alt + Z** to open the MegaWorld Window. In the **Tools Panel**, click the **+** button and choose **Pin**.

---

## Step 2 — Select which prototypes can be pinned

In the **Selection Panel**, choose the Types and prototypes you want to work with.

---

## Step 3 — Pin objects in the scene view

Click objects in the **Scene View** to pin them. Pinned objects are marked and will be skipped by brush operations (Brush Erase, Brush Modify, Brush Physics, etc.) that respect the pin flag.

To unpin an object, click it again with the Pin tool active.

---

## What's next

- Use [Precise Place](/megaworld/getting-started/precise-place) to place the hero objects you then want to pin.
- Use [Edit](/megaworld/getting-started/edit) to fine-tune a pinned object's transform before locking it in.
- Read the full [Pin](/megaworld/tools/pin) reference documentation.
