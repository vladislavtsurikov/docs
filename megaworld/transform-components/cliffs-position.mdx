---
title: "Cliffs Position"
description: "Push objects away from the cliff face along the surface normal direction"
---

Cliffs Position offsets the spawn position of an object outward from the cliff surface. This ensures objects placed on cliff faces are not buried inside the terrain geometry.

## Settings

- **Offset Position** — additional XZ offset magnitude applied on top of the normal direction offset. Increase this value if objects are still intersecting the cliff surface. Default is `1`.

<Tip>
  Use this together with [Cliffs Align](/megaworld/transform-components/cliffs-align) for the best results when placing rocks or stones on steep cliff faces.
</Tip>
