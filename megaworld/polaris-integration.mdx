You can follow the normal MegaWorld workflow with Polaris Terrain, but there is one important limitation in the original documentation.

## Recommended approach

Follow the same general setup you would use in the regular getting started workflow, but use **Simple Filters** for the filtering stage.

## Important limitation

The original MegaWorld documentation notes that **Mask Filter** support for Polaris may be limited or may not be available in the same way as on Unity Terrain.

Because of that, when you work with Polaris:

- prefer **Simple Filters** first;
- validate the result directly in the scene;
- avoid assuming that every terrain-based mask workflow from Unity Terrain behaves the same way.
